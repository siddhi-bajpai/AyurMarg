Tulsi (Holy Basil)

General Information

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is an aromatic perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family. It's revered in India for its numerous health benefits and spiritual significance. The herb has a strong aroma and is used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.

Benefits

Tulsi possesses potent antioxidant properties, supports the immune system, and aids in stress reduction. It may also help in managing respiratory disorders and promoting heart health.

Applications

It is used in various forms, including fresh leaves, dried powder, or as an extract. Tulsi is utilized in teas, supplements, and essential oils. It's commonly incorporated into Ayurvedic and herbal remedies for its therapeutic effects.

Precautions

Despite its health benefits, excessive consumption of Tulsi may have adverse effects. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should consult healthcare professionals before using it. Individuals with existing medical conditions should also seek advice.
